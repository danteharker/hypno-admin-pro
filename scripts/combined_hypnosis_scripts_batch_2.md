# Boosting Self-Esteem

## Introduction

This script is designed to help individuals enhance their self-esteem and self-worth, fostering a positive self-image and a deep sense of inner confidence. It aims to reprogram negative self-talk and cultivate self-acceptance.

## Script

**(Induction - Progressive Relaxation)**

"As you settle into a comfortable position, allow your eyes to gently close. Take a deep, calming breath, and as you exhale, release any tension you might be holding. Continue to breathe deeply and evenly, allowing each breath to carry you deeper into relaxation.

Bring your awareness to your feet, feeling them relax and grow heavy. Let this wave of relaxation move up through your ankles, calves, and knees. Feel your thighs relaxing, becoming soft and loose. This soothing sensation continues into your hips and lower back, releasing any stiffness. Your abdomen softens, and your chest relaxes with each gentle breath.

Feel the relaxation spreading up your spine, through your shoulders, and down your arms to your fingertips. Your neck and jaw relax, your facial muscles soften, and your eyelids feel wonderfully heavy. You are now deeply relaxed, peaceful, and calm."

**(Deepener - The Mirror of Truth)**

"Imagine yourself standing before a magnificent, shimmering mirror, a 'Mirror of Truth'. This mirror reflects your true essence, your inner light, and all your wonderful qualities. As you gaze into it, you see yourself with absolute clarity, free from judgment or self-criticism. You see your strengths, your kindness, your unique talents, and your inherent worth. With each moment you spend before this mirror, you feel a profound sense of self-acceptance and love, drawing you deeper into a state of unwavering self-esteem."

**(Therapeutic Content)**

"Now, as you stand before this Mirror of Truth, feel a powerful sense of self-esteem radiating from within you. You recognise that you are a unique, valuable, and capable individual, deserving of love, respect, and happiness. Any old beliefs about not being good enough, or any negative self-talk, simply dissolve and fade away, replaced by a profound sense of inner confidence.

Imagine yourself moving through your daily life with a newfound sense of self-assurance. You speak kindly to yourself, you celebrate your successes, and you learn from challenges without self-blame. You set healthy boundaries, knowing your worth, and you attract positive relationships and opportunities into your life. You trust your intuition and make decisions that align with your highest good.

From this moment forward, you will find that your self-esteem naturally grows stronger each day. You will appreciate your unique qualities, acknowledge your achievements, and treat yourself with kindness and compassion. You are building a solid foundation of self-love and self-respect that will empower you in all areas of your life. You are confident, capable, and truly worthy."

**(Termination)**

"Now, it is time to gently return to your full waking awareness, bringing with you all the positive feelings of enhanced self-esteem, inner confidence, and self-acceptance. I will count from one to five, and with each number, you will feel more and more alert, refreshed, and ready to engage with your day.

One... beginning to feel a gentle energy returning to your body.
Two... becoming more aware of your surroundings, feeling grounded and present.
Three... feeling refreshed and invigorated, carrying your boosted self-esteem with you.
Four... your mind is clear, your body is energised, and you are ready to open your eyes.
And five... eyes open, wide awake, feeling absolutely wonderful, confident, and full of self-worth."
